UCSC Computational Media

PhD Application Portfolio · Fall 2026

I've spent two decades building technology systems for organizations doing hard, human work: helping researchers improve both large- and small-scale statewide testing programs, supporting families through eating disorder caregiving and treatment, and helping an Indigenous community revitalize and maintain its language sovereignty.

Now I'm turning that experience toward a research question: how does sustained, reflective interaction with conversational AI shape the way adults think, decide, and understand themselves—especially during major life transitions?

And does it connect them to community, or substitute for it?
