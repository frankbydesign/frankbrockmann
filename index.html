Frank Brockmann

PhD Applicant, UC Santa Cruz, Computational Media (Fall 2026)

I've spent two decades building technology systems for organizations doing hard, human work: supporting families through eating disorder treatment, helping Indigenous communities revitalize and maintain language sovereignty, and working with researchers to improve both large-scale and under-resourced educational assessment programs.

Now I'm turning that experience toward a research question: how does sustained, reflective interaction with conversational AI shape the way adults think, decide, and understand themselves—especially during major life transitions? And does it connect them to community, or substitute for it?
