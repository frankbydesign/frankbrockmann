Frank Brockmann

PhD Applicant, Computational Media (Fall 2026)

I've spent 25 years building technology systems for organizations doing hard, human work—supporting families through eating disorder treatment, helping indigenous communities maintain linguistic sovereignty, improving educational assessment for under-resourced schools.

Now I'm turning that experience toward a research question: how do computational systems shape the people who use them? Not just whether the tools work, but how they change the way we think, decide, and understand ourselves.
