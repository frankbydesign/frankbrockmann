Portfolio F.E.A.S.T. Project
F.E.A.S.T. · Director of Technology Services · 2020–present

Technology Systems for Families in Crisis

F.E.A.S.T. helps families navigating eating disorder treatment—about 15,000 each year. As the only technical staff, I built and maintain the infrastructure that connects panicked parents to evidence-based support.

What I Built

Salesforce CRM migration:

Moved from a legacy system to Salesforce, designing how family interactions get tracked, searched, and followed up. Every field choice matters—what we capture shapes who we can help.

Staff dashboards:

Built reporting tools so a small team can spot patterns in thousands of help requests. Which resources do families actually use? Where do people drop off? The dashboards make those questions answerable.

Automated email sequences:

Designed the 30-day email course that's reached 17,000+ families. Timing, segmentation, content triggers—all choices about how a computational system can maintain connection during crisis.
