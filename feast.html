Technology Infrastructure for Caregiver Support

Director of Technology Services · 2020–Present

This page is under construction. More artifacts coming soon.

F.E.A.S.T. supports families navigating eating disorder treatment, about 15,000 each year. As the only technical staff, I built and maintain the systems that connect families to evidence-based resources and peer support. This work includes CRM infrastructure, analytics tools, communications systems, and organizational AI policy.
